## ğŸ§© Overview
This project is a **modern, responsive dashboard** built using **Next.js 13+ (App Router)**, **Tailwind CSS**, **Recharts**, and **shadcn/ui**. The goal is to provide a production-ready foundation for analytical dashboards involving multiple approval flows, business metrics, and real-time updates.

The dashboard is modular and easily extensible, with the following key sections:
- **My Approvals & Actions** (internal, price, PO, invoice approvals, etc.)
- **My Daily Update** (order tracking, BOM, production details)
- **My QC** (wastage analytics)
- **My Business Health Overview** (sales, clients, quotations, top-selling products)
- **Purchases Summary** (trends, vendor analysis, category-wise stats)

### 1ï¸âƒ£ My Approvals & Actions

### 3ï¸âƒ£ My QC (Quality Control)

### 4ï¸âƒ£ My Business Health Overview
- KPI tiles: Sales, Quotations, New vs Old Clients, Region-wise Sales.
- Each metric can be filtered by year/quarter/month/date range.
- Visuals: Line & Bar charts (Recharts).

### 5ï¸âƒ£ Purchases Summary
- Displays total purchases, growth trend, vendor-wise, and category-wise summary.
- Real-time tracking from `/api/purchases`.
